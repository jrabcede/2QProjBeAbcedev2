Goldfish Care

Goldfish Trivia

  • Goldfish are relatives of koi carp.

  • Goldfish are the most popular and the most abused fish in the aquarium trade, this is mainly because of the false advertising of fish bowls.

  • The oldest goldfish lived up to 43 years old.

Goldfish Keeping Tips

  1. Goldfish should be keot in waters with a pH level of around 7-8.5.

  2. Goldfish should be kept in large tanks with lots of water and no substrate, they also need oxygen in the water, like most fish, to survive.

  3. Goldfish should not be put in aquariums with many decorations because they like to dig and misplace decorations

  4. You should only feed goldfish the minimal food that they require because they can get bloated easily.

  5. Goldfish are not good tankmates because they tend to eat anything that fits their mouths and are very easy slow moving targets for larger fish.

GOLDFISH

Goldfish are also often seen in fish shows because of their varieties, sizes, humps and colors. Goldfish like larger tanks and clear but healthy water and are not suited for decorated aquariums. Many enthusiasts breed them and groom them for show. They groom the goldfish to enlargen their humps and to brighten their colors as well as enlarging their bodies. Many fish keepers often veer away from keeping goldfish because of their destructive tendencies and their incompatibility with other fish. Many goldfish breeds are subject to controversy because of their enlarged bubble eyes and cheeks.
