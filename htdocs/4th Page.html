Guppy Fish Care

Guppy Trivia

  • Guppies give birth to live young

  • Guppies can give birth to about 30-100 babies in one pregnancy

  • Male guppies have a gonopodium, a fin structure that acts like a penis.

Guppy Keeping Tips

  1. If you do not want an overstocked aquarium, ypu should kep female guppies separate from males.

  2. Guppies can tolerate a water pH of 5.5-8.5.

  3. Guppies like lots of vegetation in their environment because they also feed upon tiny organisms and algae that reside near plants.

  4. You should also separate Adult guppies from their babies because they tend to eat their own babies.

  5. Guppies like warmer waters, this makes mthem more active and they metabolize faster.

GUPPIES

Guppies are probably the most well known and abundant starter fish. Guppies can withstand harsh aquarium conditions and still reproduce like crazy, a community of about a few hundred guppies will have hundreds of babies every week. Guppies are also usually seen in fish shows because of their colors, fins and patterns. There are hundreds of guppy strains around the world, each with their own unique pattern and are classified into groups according to their color, pattern and fin forms. Breeders have selectively bred guppies for new patterns and fin shapes for decades, the reason why some guppies are getting less hardy is because of excessive inbreeding to stabilize a pattern.
